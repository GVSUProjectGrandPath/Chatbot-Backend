Saving Tips and Tricks
        When it comes to saving money, it’s all about deciding where your money will be kept the safest and how you can add to it.
        You could open a savings account. A good high-yield savings account can earn you a lot of interest. A certificate of deposit would also grow your money, but with penalties for taking that money out too soon.
        Or you could use the envelope method and divide your money into separate categories like rent, groceries, and savings. This controls your spending and ensures savings goals are met. 
        Finally you could set up automatic transfers and have your money save itself. This ensures consistent savings growth and removes your temptation to spend.
        However you save your money, it’s best to start as soon as possible. Saving isn’t about how much you make, but about how well you manage it.
