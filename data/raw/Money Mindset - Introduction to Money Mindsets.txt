Money Mindset: Introduction to Money Mindsets
"Before we can teach you anything about money, you should first understand your relationship to money."



What is a Money Mindset?
A money mindset refers to the beliefs, attitudes, and emotions that shape how an individual approaches earning, saving, spending, and investing money. It influences financial behaviors, decision-making, and long-term financial well-being. Understanding your money mindset helps identify strengths, challenges, and areas for growth in financial habits.
